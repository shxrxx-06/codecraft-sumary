About CodeCraft!

This is a Technical Event on mastering flask organised by AI students under the guidance of the faculties. This was a 3 day event conducted from July 22(Monday) to July 24(Wednesday).First day was quiz round also qualifying round which was conducted in three batches and students were shortlisted for second round Mastering Flask. 65 students out of 98 were shortlisted and they were divided into teams and worked on creating a website with it's basics attended the workshop on website creation on the next two days!.
